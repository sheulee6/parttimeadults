PartTime Adults is a creative studio. Located in the heart of the JCCAC arts community (L4-12), this space serves as a creative laboratory for visual storytelling, conceptual art, and community archiving.

We believe that being a "full-time adult" shouldn’t mean losing touch with your community, your curiosity, or your city. Our practice sits at the intersection of people, memory, and space. By shifting the lens from personal healing to broader social connection, the studio creates art that triggers awareness, preserves local memories, and fosters deep human relationships.

Whether through multi-sensory workshops, local illustrations, or cross-cultural collaborations, PartTime Adults is dedicated to presenting the unique cultural value of Hong Kong to both locals and the global stage.

This is a space for art, but more importantly, it is an open platform for fellow dreamers and anyone who cares deeply about this city.

Our Artistic Core & Practice

Socially Engaged Art

We don't believe art is confined to a gallery wall. We use diverse mediums—from illustration and photography to sensory gathering—tailored to specific social topics and communities.

The Five Senses Approach

Moving beyond the visual, we design interactive experiences (like our past Waves After Wind project) using sound, scent, and touch to break language barriers and connect neighbors.

18 Districts Narrative

We are committed to documenting the overlooked corners and shifting landscapes of Hong Kong—celebrating the beauty of the ordinary.
