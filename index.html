We believe that being a "full-time adult" shouldn’t mean losing touch with your community, your curiosity, or your city.

我們堅信，成為一個「全職大人」，不意味著必須與社區、好奇心或這座城市失去連結。

Our practice sits at the intersection of people, memory, and space. The studio creates art that triggers awareness, preserves local memories, and fosters deep human relationships.

我們的實踐立足於人、記憶與空間的交匯處。工作室致力於創作能喚醒意識、保存本地記憶並深化人際關係的藝術作品。

Located in the heart of the JCCAC arts community, PartTime Adults is a creative laboratory. This is a space for art, but more importantly, it is an open platform for fellow dreamers and anyone who cares deeply about this city.

「兼職大人」座落於 JCCAC 藝術村的核心，是一個創意實驗室。這不僅是一個藝術空間，更是一個為同路人及深愛這座城市的人所建立的開放平台。

Core 01

Socially Engaged

社會參與

We don't believe art is confined to a gallery wall. We use diverse mediums—from illustration to sensory gathering—tailored to specific social topics.

我們不認為藝術僅局限於畫廊之內。針對特定社會議題，我們運用從插畫到感官聚會等多元媒介進行創作。

Core 02

Five Senses

五感體驗

Moving beyond the visual, we design interactive experiences using sound, scent, and touch to break language barriers and connect neighbors.

超越視覺的侷限，我們透過聲音、氣味與觸覺設計互動體驗，打破語言隔閡並重新連結社區鄰里。

Core 03

18 Districts

十八區紀錄

Committed to documenting the overlooked corners and shifting landscapes of Hong Kong—celebrating the beauty of the ordinary.

致力於紀錄香港被忽視的角落與不斷變遷的地景——頌揚尋常事物中的不凡之美。
